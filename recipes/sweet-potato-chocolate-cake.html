Sweet Potato Chocolate Cake

Description

A decadent, moist chocolate cake with only 3 ingredients.

Try it out!

Ingredients

Cake

  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 200 gr of chocolate of choice

Glaze

  • Â½ cup coconut milk
  • 200 gr of chocolate of choice

Steps

  1. Peel sweet potatoes and dice them
  2. Boil them until soft
  3. Mix them in a blender or by hand
  4. Melt 200 gr of chocolate (use coconut oil, if using microwave)
  5. Add the melted chocolate to the sweet potato mix and stir
  6. Put in a form to cool down
  7. Melt chocolate and mix with the coconut milk
  8. Pour the mix over the cake
  9. Set aside and let cool down
  10. Enjoy!
